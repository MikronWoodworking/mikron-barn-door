INSTALLATION PROCESS

Quick & Professional Install

Designed for contractors who need efficient, repeatable installations

1

Mount the Track

Track hangers are independent from the track, allowing anchoring directly on studs or weight-bearing support. Pre-drill holes directly into the studs and mount track hangers. Slotted hanger holes allow up to 1/8" adjustment for proper leveling. Spacers included for 1/8" increments up to 7/8" adjustment from wall. Weight limit up to 125lbs per track hanger when lag screws are fastened to studs. Maximum 24" between track hangers recommended. Standard 80" aluminum track can easily be cut to size.

Hang the Door
